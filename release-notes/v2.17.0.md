## v2.17.0

### New Features
- **chat**: Hold-to-record voice messages in DMs and channels (drag-up to lock; requires send/Enter to deliver)
- **dm**: Call history log in DMs — missed, declined and completed P2P calls now appear with duration
- **soundboard**: Live volume control during playback, draggable trim region, manual start/end time inputs, and a sidebar play indicator
- **voice**: Stop-watching button on each screen-share window (appears on hover, auto-hides when idle)
- **voice**: Bind push-to-talk, mute and deafen to mouse side buttons
- **sidebar**: Scrollable Friends and DM sections with max-height; removed the friends-list cap
- **friends**: DM unread badge now shows on friends-list rows
- **help**: In-app Help Center — searchable articles with screenshots/clips, full EN + TR translations, accessible from Settings and the landing page
- **release-notes**: In-app and landing-page release notes viewer

### Bug Fixes
- **voice**: Stop the call timer when a channel empties, with a LiveKit reconciliation backstop (fixes runaway channel durations)
- **voice**: Show in-progress voice participants to users who join a server mid-call
- **voice**: Clear the stale green speaking ring when a user leaves the channel
- **voice**: Make PTT and mute/deafen work while the app is focused
- **voice**: Cancel a deferred outside-click listener that closed the device picker popup in dev
- **soundboard**: Enforce the 7-second trim limit server-side and stop playback when leaving voice
