## Storage

See how much **storage** your uploads use against your quota.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the storage usage indicator.

## Deleted servers

Servers you delete aren't gone right away — they're **recoverable for 30 days** from the deleted-servers list before they're permanently removed. Handy if you change your mind.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the deleted-servers list with a restore option.

## Delete your account

Deleting your account (confirmed with your password) is a **soft delete**: it stays **recoverable for 30 days** — just sign back in within that window to restore it. After 30 days it's permanently erased.
