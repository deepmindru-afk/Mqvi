# Create an account & sign in

Getting into mqvi takes under a minute.

## Create an account

1. Open the app and choose **Register**.
2. Pick a **username** and a strong **password**.
3. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
4. You're in — a one-time welcome screen points out the basics.

> Email is **optional**. You can add one later in **Account Settings** to enable password recovery.

## Sign in

1. Choose **Log In** and enter your username and password.
2. On desktop, tick **Remember me** to stay signed in (credentials are stored encrypted by your OS).

## Forgot your password?

If you added an email, use **Forgot password** on the login screen to receive a reset link. Without an email, an admin of your server can help you recover access.
