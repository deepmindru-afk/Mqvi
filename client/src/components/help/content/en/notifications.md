# Notifications

## Mute what you don't need

Mute a whole **server** or an individual **channel** — for an hour, eight hours, a week, or forever. Muted places stop pinging you but still show as unread.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the mute-duration menu on a server or channel.

## Mentions & badges

You're still alerted when someone **@mentions** you or **replies**, even in muted channels. Unread counts and a **taskbar badge** (desktop) keep track of what's waiting.

## Sounds

Adjust the **notification** and **app sound** volumes in **Voice settings**, including the subtle voice **join/leave** sounds.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the notification and app-sound volume sliders.
