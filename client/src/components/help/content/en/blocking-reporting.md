# Blocking & reporting

## Block someone

**Block** a user to stop their direct messages and hide them from you. You can **unblock** any time from your blocked-users list in settings.

![the block action and the blocked-users list](assets/blocking-reporting-1.webm)

## Report a user

If someone breaks the rules, **report** them with a **reason** — spam, harassment, inappropriate content, impersonation, or other — a short description, and optional **screenshots** as evidence. Reports go to the platform admins.

![the report dialog with reason, description, and evidence upload](assets/blocking-reporting-2.png)
