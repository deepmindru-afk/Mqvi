# Welcome to mqvi

mqvi is a self-hostable chat platform for **text, voice, video, and screen sharing** — with end-to-end encryption built in. This guide walks you through everything the app can do, from your first message to running your own server.

## What you can do here

- **Talk** — text channels, direct messages, and high-quality voice/video calls
- **Share** — files, images, and your screen (with system audio)
- **Organize** — servers, channels, roles, and permissions
- **Stay private** — optional end-to-end encryption for messages, files, and calls
