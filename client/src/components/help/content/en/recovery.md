# Don't lose access — recovery

Because encryption keys live on your devices, you need a way back in if you reinstall or switch devices.

## Set a recovery password

Set a **recovery password** to securely back up your keys. Keep it somewhere safe — it's what unlocks your encrypted history.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the set-recovery-password dialog.

## Restore on a new device

Signing in somewhere new? Choose **restore from recovery** and enter your recovery password to bring your encrypted messages with you. Without it, a new device starts fresh and can't read your old encrypted messages.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the restore-from-recovery prompt.
