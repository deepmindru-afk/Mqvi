# Mentions

Get someone's attention by mentioning them.

## Mention a person or role

Type **`@`** and start typing a name — a list pops up. Pick a **person** to ping them directly, or a **role** to ping everyone who has it. Mentioned users get a notification and a highlight, even if the channel is muted.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the @-mention autocomplete list while typing.

## Where your mentions show up

Mentions you receive are tracked so you never miss one — unread counts and badges point you to channels where you were mentioned.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** a channel with an unread mention badge.
