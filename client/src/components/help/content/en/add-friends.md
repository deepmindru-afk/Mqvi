# Friends

## Add a friend

Open the **Friends** view, search for someone by username, and send a **friend request**. It shows up under their pending requests until they respond.

![the add-friend search box in the Friends view](assets/add-friends-1.png)

## Requests & tabs

The Friends view has tabs — **All**, **Online**, and **Pending** (incoming and outgoing). **Accept** or **decline** requests sent to you, or **cancel** ones you sent.

![the Pending tab with incoming and outgoing requests](assets/add-friends-2.png)

## Remove a friend

You can **remove** a friend any time from their profile card or the friends list.
