# Permissions

Permissions decide what members can do — send messages, manage channels, kick or ban, connect to voice, use the soundboard, and more. The **Admin** permission grants everything.

## Server-wide

A role's permissions apply across the whole server. Give trusted roles more; keep the default role limited.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the permission toggles for a role.

## Per-channel overrides

On any channel you can **override** permissions for specific roles or members — **allow** or **deny** something just there. For example:

- make one channel **read-only**
- open a voice channel to a **single role**
- hide a channel from everyone but a team

Admin always bypasses overrides.
