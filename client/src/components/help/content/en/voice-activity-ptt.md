# Voice activity vs push-to-talk

Choose how your microphone transmits — **Settings → Voice → input mode**.

## Voice activity (open mic)

Your mic transmits **automatically when you speak**. Set the **sensitivity** so quiet background noise doesn't trigger it but your voice always does. The mic test helps you find the right threshold.

![the voice-activity sensitivity slider](assets/voice-activity-ptt-1.png)

## Push-to-talk

Your mic transmits **only while you hold a key**. Bind any key you like — on the **desktop app** it works even when mqvi isn't the focused window (a global shortcut), so it's perfect for noisy rooms or shared spaces.

![the push-to-talk key binding control](assets/voice-activity-ptt-2.png)
