# Status & presence

Let people know whether you're around.

## Your status

mqvi sets you to **online**, **idle**, or **offline** automatically. You can also set it by hand:

- **Online** — available
- **Idle** — away from the keyboard
- **Do Not Disturb** — online but suppressing notifications
- **Invisible** — appear offline while still using the app

> 📸 **Screenshot:** `assets/status-presence-1.webp` — the status menu on your user avatar.

## Custom status

Add a short **custom status** message that others see on your profile and in the member list.
