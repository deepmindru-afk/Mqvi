# A tour of the interface

Here's what's on screen, left to right.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** `assets/interface-tour-1.webp` — the full app window with each region labelled.

## Left — servers & channels

The sidebar lists your **servers** and, under the active one, its **channels** (grouped into categories). Click a **text** channel to chat or a **voice** channel to talk. You can collapse the sidebar down to a compact icon rail.

## Center — the channel

The main area shows the active channel: messages with the composer at the bottom, or — in a voice channel — the participant grid and any screen shares.

## Right — members

The member list shows who's in the server, grouped by role and online status. On the **Friends** view it switches to show which of your friends are currently in a voice channel.

## Top — title bar (desktop)

The desktop title bar holds the **ⓘ info** button (this guide and release notes), the **Feedback** button, and the window controls.

## Bottom-left — you

Your avatar, name, and quick **mute / deafen / settings** controls sit at the bottom of the sidebar.
