# Password & email

Open **Settings → Security**.

## Password

Change your password any time — enter your current one and a new one.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the change-password form.

## Email (optional)

Add an **email** to enable **password recovery**, or change/remove it later. Email changes ask for your password to confirm it's really you.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the email section in Security settings.
