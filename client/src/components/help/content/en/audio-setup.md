# Audio setup

Get your microphone and speakers dialed in — open **Settings → Voice**.

## Devices

Choose your **input** (microphone) and **output** (speaker or headset) devices. If you only have one of each, mqvi uses it automatically.

![the input/output device dropdowns in Voice settings](assets/audio-setup-1.png)

## Input volume & mic test

Set your **input volume**, then run the **mic test** to watch your level and hear yourself back — so you know others will hear you clearly.

![the mic test meter reacting to speech](assets/audio-setup-2.png)

## Noise reduction

Turn on **noise reduction** to filter out keyboard clatter, fans, and background hum before your voice goes out. It runs entirely on your device, and you can toggle it off any time.

![the noise reduction toggle](assets/audio-setup-3.png)
