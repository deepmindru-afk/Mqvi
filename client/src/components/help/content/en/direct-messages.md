# Direct messages

Private one-on-one conversations, separate from any server.

## Start a DM

Open someone's **profile** and choose message, or click them in your friends list. Your open conversations appear in the **DM list**.

![the DM list with a conversation open](assets/direct-messages-1.png)

## Organize

**Pin** a conversation to keep it at the top, **mute** it (1 hour, 8 hours, a week, or forever), **mark it read**, or **search** within it.

![the DM right-click menu with pin / mute / search](assets/direct-messages-2.png)

## Message requests

DMs from people you're **not friends with** arrive as **message requests** — accept to start chatting, or decline. You control who's allowed to message you in your DM privacy settings.

![an incoming message request with accept / decline](assets/direct-messages-3.png)
