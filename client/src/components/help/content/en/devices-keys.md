# Devices & verification

Encryption is tied to your **devices**, each with its own keys.

## Your devices

In **Encryption settings**, see every device signed in to your account and when each was last active. **Remove** any you don't recognize to cut off its access.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** `assets/devices-keys-1.webp` — the device list with "this device" and a remove action.

## Verify someone

Each person has a **key fingerprint**. Compare it with a contact — in person or over another channel — to confirm you're really talking to them and not an impostor. Verified contacts are marked as **verified**.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** `assets/devices-keys-2.webp` — a key fingerprint with the verified / unverified state.
