# Desktop app settings

**Settings → General** (desktop only).

## Startup & tray

- **Open at login** — launch mqvi when your computer starts
- **Start minimized** — open quietly to the system tray
- **Close to tray** — keep running in the background when you close the window

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the startup and tray toggles.

## Look

Enable a **transparent background** for a frosted-glass window (a restart applies it).

## Shortcuts

Bind **global mute and deafen** keys that work even when mqvi isn't the focused window.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** the global shortcut bindings.

## Updates

The app updates itself — when a new version is ready, click the **Update** button in the title bar to restart into it.
